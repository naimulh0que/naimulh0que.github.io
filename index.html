nhoque@umd.edu

Google Scholar | Github

I am a second-year PhD student at the College of Information Studies (iSchool), University of Maryland, College Park, where I work with Professor Niklas Elmqvist and a member of the Human-Computer Interaction Lab. My current research interests are Human-centered AI and Visual Analytics. In general, I am enthusiastic about designing tools for supporting human-AI collaboration. I obtained a Masters in Computer Science from Stony Brook University where I worked and still collaborate with Professor Klaus Mueller. Before that, I obtained a BSc in Computer Science from University of Dhaka.
