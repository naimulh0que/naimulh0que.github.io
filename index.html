nhoque@umd.edu

Google Scholar | Github

I am a third-year PhD student at the College of Information Studies (iSchool), University of Maryland, College Park, where I work with Professor Niklas Elmqvist and a member of the Human-Computer Interaction Lab. My current research interest is combining Artificial Intelligence and Information Visualization to help people learn, create, and make sense of complex and abstract tasks (e.g., fiction writing). I obtained a Masters in Computer Science from Stony Brook University where I worked and still collaborate with Professor Klaus Mueller. I also collaborate with Professor Syed Billah (Penn State) and Professor Naeemul Hassan (UMD) regularly. I obtained a BSc in Computer Science from University of Dhaka, Bangladesh. You can call me Naimul (pronunciation: na-ei-mul).
